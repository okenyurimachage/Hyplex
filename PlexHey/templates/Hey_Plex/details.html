Our Rental Terms


  1. BOOKING

    2. A client can book more than one vehicle before the pickup date.After the pickup date he/she must return the lended vehicle before booking another one.


  2. DRIVING LICENSE

    3. A client must have a valid driving license.He/She must be over 21 years old. The client must also have a identification card.


  3. RENTAL AGREEMENT

    4. In order to check out the rental vehicle, the rental agreement mst be signed time of pickup.The contract is offered both in English and Kiswahili.This contract deals with essential information such as the xar type, rental period, insurance coverage etc.


  4. CANCELLATION

    5. A client cannot cancel the reserved vehicle after making the first deposit.


  1. DEPOSIT

    2. A client must deposit 50% of the amount of money needed to hire a vehicle for the specified number of days. The remaining amount will be paid when acquiring the vehicle in our offices. All payments are done through MPesa.


  2. FUEL

    3. Renting costs do not include fuel


  3. INSURANCE

    4. All vehicles are insured but any irresponsible damage e.g. loss of vehicle properties will be catered by the client. The client must take care of the vehicle to avoid theft.If the vehicle is stolen, He/She will cater for all the expenses required to track the vehicle
